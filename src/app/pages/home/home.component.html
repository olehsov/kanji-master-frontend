About KanjiMaster

KanjiMaster is a web application designed to help users improve their Japanese reading and writing skills through interactive flashcards and quizzes.

Our mission is to provide users with a fun and engaging way to learn and practice their kanji characters, which are the building blocks of Japanese language.

With KanjiMaster, users can easily search for and study kanji characters, view their meanings and readings, and practice writing them out in context.

We also offer interactive quizzes to test users' knowledge of kanji, and create custom study plans to suit individual learning styles.

KanjiMaster is a free and open-source web application, available for use on desktop and mobile devices.
